Olive oil is a great component in salad dressing, and it's benefits don't end there! Since ancient times, people have taken advantage of the skin-softening properties of this oil.

When blended with lye to make soap, olive oil contributes to smoother, softer, more elastic skin. It cleanses your skin without stripping away the natural oils, allows the skin to 'breathe', sweat, and shed cells naturally.

Glycerin, which is a byproduct of the soapmaking process, is retained in all of our olive oil soaps. Highly moisturising it can actually absorb water from the air! Commercial makers usually remove glycerin to use in other things. With our soaps you get the best of both amazing ingredients!

The aim of our products is to leave you soft, supple and glowing. All our fragrances are essential oils. From, lavendar to relax, to tea-tree to invigorate. These soothe the soul, whilst the soap looks after your skin. Choose your favourite below!
